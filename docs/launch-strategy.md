## Product Hunt Launch

### Preparation
- **Day**: Monday (highest traffic day)
- **Time**: 12:01 AM PT (full 24-hour window)
- **Tagline**: "AI-powered marketing across 9 platforms -- open source"
- **Description**: Focus on the problem (platform blindness) and solution (cross-platform AI)

### Assets Needed
- GIF demo (15 seconds): Connect platform -> Generate content -> Publish -> See analytics
- Screenshots: Dashboard, agent trace, analytics comparison
- Logo + social preview image

### Maker Comment Template
```
Hey PH! I built OrchestraAI because I was frustrated managing
marketing across 5 different platforms.

Each platform has its own analytics, its own "smart" bidding,
and none of them talk to each other.

OrchestraAI connects all your platforms and uses AI agents to
optimize across them. It knows where your next $1 will produce
the best return.

Key features:
- 9 platform connectors
- LangGraph multi-agent AI
- Guardrailed bidding (your money is safe)
- Cross-platform intelligence (the moat)
- Open source (Apache 2.0)

I'd love your feedback! Happy to answer any questions.
```

## Hacker News Strategy

### "Show HN" Post

**Title**: "Show HN: OrchestraAI -- Open-source AI marketing orchestration across 9 platforms"

**Text**: Focus on technical architecture:
- LangGraph for stateful multi-agent orchestration
- Thompson Sampling for content A/B testing
- Differential privacy for aggregate model
- 14 hard-coded restrictions for ethical AI
- Kill switch for instant spend halt

**What HN readers care about**:
- Technical depth (agent architecture, safety system)
- Open source (Apache 2.0, self-hostable)
- Ethical AI (guardrails, transparency, restrictions)
- Novel approach (cross-platform intelligence moat)
